# BullOrBS Strategy

## Core Moat: SEO
- Must rank for EVERY stock search in Canadian market
- Programmatic SEO: pages for hundreds of tickers
- Long-tail targeting: "should I buy [TICKER]", "[TICKER] stock analysis 2026"
- "Made in Canada, for everyone" positioning

## Satire Targets (not just Motley Fool)
- Motley Fool Canada (fool.ca)
- Seeking Alpha
- Zacks
- The Globe and Mail stock picks
- BNN Bloomberg recommendations
- Any popular newsletter making public picks

## Content Rules
- Every recommendation MUST have facts + reasoning + references
- Show data sources for every claim
- Full elimination tournament (10-15 candidates → 1 winner)
- Grade competing newsletters' picks with data

## Design Direction
- Lightweight, fast (Core Web Vitals = SEO ranking factor)
- Eye-catching for ALL ages (not just tech/young crowd)
- Clean, accessible, not dark-hacker aesthetic
- Think: Bloomberg meets Morning Brew

## SEO Pages Needed
- /stock/[ticker] — programmatic pages for every major TSX + US stock
- /roast/[slug] — satire audits of competitor picks
- /pick/[slug] — AI weekly picks with reasoning
- /compare/[ticker-vs-ticker] — comparison pages (SEO gold)
- /newsletter-review/[name] — review pages for competing newsletters
